Bagel's Dragon's Venom Wins Award for "Most Dangerous BBQ Sauce"

The ARA has given Bagel's famous and possibly infamous sauce, Dragon's Venom, the 2019 award for "Most Dangerous BBQ Sauce." This really should be illegal, but we got an award for it so it must be fine.

Boy Becomes First to Survive Consumption of Bagel's Dragon's Venom Sauce

13 year old Brock Bud became the first human to consume a full order of wings(12) lathered in the Bagel's notorious Dragon's Venom sauce, just after the award was given to the sauce for being the "Most Dangerous BBQ Sauce." Brock did have to report to the hospital with intense stomach pain after eating all the wings, though he was released later that day.

Bagel's First Gift Shop Opens Today!

Bagel's BBQ Sauce has opened up their first gift shop as of today! The first 20 customers received a complimentary bottle of Honey Mustard Sauce, and the first 5 also received a miniature bottle of Dragon's Venom, probably for souvenier rather than consumption. The store features T-Shirts, stuffed animals, blankets, and of course, the special Bagel's Sauces.
